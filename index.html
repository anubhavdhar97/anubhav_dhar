        <h2 class="text-4xl font-bold text-center text-indigo-700 mb-10">About Me</h2>

        <div class="flex flex-col lg:flex-row items-center lg:space-x-8">

            <div class="lg:w-1/2 mb-8 lg:mb-0">

                <img src="uploaded:ad-pass-pic.jpg-59e6cd77-a23b-49e7-be63-caca35c4d669" alt="Anubhav Dhar's Photo" class="rounded-lg shadow-lg object-cover w-full h-auto">

            </div>

            <div class="lg:w-1/2 text-lg leading-relaxed">

                <p class="mb-4">

                    I am a PhD student in the Department of Biochemistry at the Indian Institute of Science (IISc) Bangalore. My research is primarily focused on **cell biology and the cytoskeleton**, specifically investigating **actin and septin networks** and associated proteins like tropomyosin.

                </p>

                <p class="mb-4">

                    A key aspect of my work involves developing and optimizing **cutting-edge advanced microscopy techniques**, such as **Expansion Microscopy (ExM)** and novel probes, to visualize cellular structures at super-resolution. I am dedicated to pushing the boundaries of cellular imaging and dissecting molecular mechanisms.

                </p>

                <p>

                    My professional interests lie in understanding the **fundamental principles of cell organization and division**, with a strong emphasis on **molecular mechanisms** and **tool development** for impactful research. My skills include advanced microscopy, biochemical analysis, and rigorous experimental design.

                </p>

            </div>
